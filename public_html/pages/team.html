Team

Ms. Mamta Singh, CEO

Ms. Mamta is a Engineering graduate from the University of Allahabad, majoring in Agricultural Engineering. Thereafter she completed her Masters of Engineering from the esteemed Rajasthan Agricultural University, Bikaner with a specialization in Water Resource Management. Mamta brings 18 years of extensive experience in the developmental sector with renowned organisation like TERI, UNICEF, INGOs, specifically in the areas of Water, Sanitation, Education, Health, MCH and Disability. Her career is of over 7 years with World Bank project SWAJAL, as a Project Manager. In 2004 he transitioned to UNICEF supported Consultant to RGNDWM, Govt. of India and worked in different capacities across India, through which she contributed to the development of national and global organisational policies. Her career has spanned at pan India level. Through her role in various boards and advisory panels, Mamta has shared her experiences at several national & international forums and has various awards and research publications to her credit. Mamta is happily married and has a son aged 8 years.



Hon’ble Mrs. Justice Poonam Srivastava

Mrs. Poonam Shrivastava is the Retired Judge, from Allahabad High Court. She has Graduated in Science and Law in the year 1970, enrolled as an Advocate in the year 1972 and practised in Civil, Criminal and Writ matters at Allahabad High Court. She was elevated as Additional Judge on 07/01/2004. She took oath as Permanent Judge on 18/08/2005. She has a rich experience in serving Allahabad and Jharkhand High Court.



Mrs. Arjumand Zaidi

Mrs. Arjumand Zaidi is a veteran social worker and an eminent educationist with a vision to empower the Economic Weaker Section of the society. She is the Founding Director of St. Xavier's Convent School, Gomtinagar and also running an NGO Holy Vision International. She started "Operation Light" and "Operation Hale" which is an adult literacy programme for women and health programme under which health camps respectively. She received an award by South Asia chapter of human rights as "Human Rights Educator 2016"



Mr. Madhav Kumar Kulshreshtha

Mr. Kulshreshtha comes with over 38 years’ successful track record in the education sector specially with Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan where he was retired as ASSISTANT COMMISSIONER in Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan. Over the years, he has pioneered in management, teaching and administration of school activities. Mr. Kulshreshtha has a Master’s degree in Science from Agra University and has done Masters in Education from Annamalai University. He has various awards and certificate in education sector to his credit.

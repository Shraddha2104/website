    Students Award:

    Criteria:

    • Maximum students participation from the school in the contest.

    Conditions:

    1. Only those schools shall be eligible where participation is minimum 500 students.
    2. In case of tie, the school which has produced maximum number of ranks shall be given higher ranking.

    School Coordinator’s Award:

    Criteria:

    • Maximum students participation from his/her school in the contest.

    Conditions:

    1. Overall Coordination of event in the school.
    2. Registration Process as per Terms & Conditions of the event.
    3. Students/TTHPL Problems/Issues/Grievance handling & timely solution.
    4. Smooth correspondence/communication with TTHPL & Students.

    Important Note:

    1. Fees once paid, neither be refunded under any circumstances nor be transferable to any other candidate.
    2. Management reserves right to change/ cancel date and venue due to unforeseen events.

    4. The Incentive amount shall be paid after the completion of contest.
    5. 1st, 2nd and 3rd position holder will also be awarded with the discounted rate for the events (trainings, workshops, motivational speech, educational trips etc) organized by TTHPL under the programme of INSAF.
    6. Events organised under INSAF at TTHPL will be cost modestly.
    7. For any dispute, the jurisdiction of Mumbai (Maharashtra) shall be applicable.
    8. Participation Certificates will be provided to each participant.
    9. Analytical summary prepared for the candidate year after year.
    10. Certificate of Appreciation will be presented to all the schools.

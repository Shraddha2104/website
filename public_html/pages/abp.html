ACCEPTANCE LETTER For Associate Business Partner

Theme of the Organization: TTHPL is an iconic organization and hallmark of global excellence, pioneering into the realm concerning national interest making all efforts to tap the versatile talent at grass root level. Since inception organization has grown both in grace and grandeur and firmly believe that from it portals will emerge ‘Leaders of Nation’, Scientists, Bureaucrats, Sportsmen, Researchers, Creative writers, Artists, Corporate honchos, Mountaineers, Cosmonauts and Olympic champions etc who will demonstrate the essence of talents by vindicating the faith and vision of the organization. Their achievements will be showcased in every echelon of the nation.

Responsibilities:

1.Approach head of the schools/institutions and teachers to introduce the TTHPL in their schools and motivate their students to participate in this examination.

3.Developing healthy relationship with school.
4.Facilitating TTHPL all the activities for conducting contest.
5.Ensuring smooth progress for conducting contest.

7.Responsible for getting critical feedback from the partners and students.
